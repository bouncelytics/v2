Convert lost visitors into customers.

A significant amount of your visitors forget about your website after switching to a different tab. With RemindPad, you can easily regain their attention and convert your lost visitors.

RemindPad is currently in active development. Please sign up to get notified when it's ready.

How It Works?
1. RemindPad tracks when your visitors switch to another website or when they minimize the browser.
2. If they are away for certain time from your website, we show a small notification with sound.
3. Like Facebook, users immediately notice it and come back to check what's new. Now they see your offer and regain interest in the product.
